IM-G1-U3-L17: Make 10 to Add. This discussion is anchored in the Illustrative Mathematics Grade 1, Unit 3, Lesson 17 teacher guide. The lesson purpose says students decompose one addend in order to make a ten.

Children are working on adding within 20 when one addend is 9. The teacher guide names two central pieces of work: students use the 10-frame structure to make a ten, and student methods are recorded with equations.

This discussion asks you to protect the child strategy without turning it into a rule the teacher performs for the child.
