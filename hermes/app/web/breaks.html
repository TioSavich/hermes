critical mathematics · where it breaks

Built to break

Where does arithmetic come from, for a child? Lakoff and Núñez's answer: from the body. Collecting objects into piles. Putting things into containers and taking them out. Walking along a path. Building towers and taking them apart. Each of these activities becomes a grounding metaphor — a systematic mapping from something a body knows how to do onto something arithmetic means.

Every one of these metaphors breaks. Object collection grounds 3 + 4 beautifully and then meets 3 − 7, and there is no pile of negative four stones. The break is not a defect in the child. It is a structural fact about the metaphor, and it sits exactly where the hardest teaching lives. Mathematics history answered each break with a named repair — a new metaphor patched over the hole — and children must make the same repairs, usually without anyone telling them that the ground has shifted.

The cards below are read live from the knowledge base: each metaphor's mapping, the inferences where it breaks and why, and the repair metaphors that restore closure.

The metaphor breaks above are read as facts. The two below you run yourself. Each posts to the live Hermes server and reads back what the formalism records, including the point where it records a gap in place of a result.

Commitment without entitlement

deontic scorekeeping · learner/deontic_scorekeeper.pl

Cross-multiplication returns the right number. A learner who runs it on ¾ of ⅖ has undertaken a commitment to the result 6/20 without being entitled to it: the move deploys none of the area-model account that would say why that arithmetic answers the question. Undertake the commitment and the scorekeeper records the gap: the answer is right and its justification is absent. Deposit the area-model account and the gap clears. The seed table is small; what it records is the Brandomian distinction, not a general theory of justification.

The sequent witness

proof boundary · crosswalk/families/cw_sequent_proof.pl

The witness reports which loaded prover layer accepts a sequent, and on what rule. The reflexive identity goes through on every layer. Change the right-hand side so no loaded prover derives it, and the witness says so rather than inventing a derivation. A deeper boundary belongs to the embodied prover: when a derivation touches the arche-trace, it returns erasure in place of a proof, recording that its justification gives out there. The preset below sends a sequent whose derivation draws the trace in through the pragmatic axiom pack (pml/pragmatic_axioms.pl); the prover answers erasure(propagation), and the card renders that answer in place of a derivation. A worker loaded without that pack answers that no loaded layer derives the same sequent, and the card renders that instead.

Entailments under a richer incompatibility relation

seed relation → registry pack → restored seed relation

Run the same entailment witness before and after loading the registry incompatibility pack. The pack adds recorded incompatibilities. That richer relation can also withdraw entailments licensed by the sparse seed, so more data can subtract as well as add. The final step restores the default disabled state.

  1. Run the seed relation

  2. Enrich the relation

    Enable pack(registry_incompatibility), which loads the recorded pairs.

  3. Ask again, compare, and restore

Research wing — live, in progress

    
These files are described here without loading them into the console worker.

    

      
Base-invariance demonstrations

        formalization/synthesis/run_add.pl runs the make-base addition demonstration;
        formalization/synthesis/run_lazy.pl runs the cost-bounded subtraction demonstration;
        formalization/synthesis/run_synth.pl runs every declared synthesis target.
        Each harness executes only when invoked as a command-line main, so none is eager-loaded.
        formalization/synthesis/synth.pl is the bounded program synthesizer library, and
        formalization/synthesis/synth_lazy.pl is its cost-bounded, base-parameterized library.
      

      
Lazy axiom control

        tools/axiom_toggle.pl provides reversible runtime toggles. The
        axiom_toggle operation loads it on demand because its full loader would create
        import collisions during worker startup.
      

      
Contextual axiom packs

        These packs are in force where relevant and can be switched off where they are not.
        pack(geometry) carries formalization/axioms_geometry.pl's finite
        quadrilateral incompatibilities; pack(number_theory) carries
        formalization/axioms_number_theory.pl's Euclid self-defeat argument; and
        pack(robinson) carries formalization/axioms_robinson.pl's Robinson Q
        and grounded arithmetic.
      

      
Registry-scale incompatibility

        pack(registry_incompatibility) controls the reversible
        arche-trace/registry_incompatibility_adapter.pl: loading 342 recorded
        misconception incompatibility pairs enriches the relation and withdraws some entailments
        the sparse seeds licensed.
