# Notice and Wonder
_Anchor ID: `IM-G3-U8-L12` — Grade grade3, Unit 8, Lesson 12_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create (in writing) and present a Notice and Wonder routine involving equal groups.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s create a "Notice and Wonder" routine.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to apply their understanding of equal groups to create a Notice and Wonder routine.

Lesson Synthesis
“What important things about your image did you consider as you created your Notice and Wonder?” (We thought about
whether the image shows equal groups and whether others would see the same groups that we saw. We thought about
possible questions others might ask and whether they would be about the groups or something else.)

“If you had a chance to revise your Notice and Wonder or create a new one, what would you do differently?” (Choose an
image with more things to notice, or with something missing or something curious. Think about whether others might
notice things about math in the image.)
