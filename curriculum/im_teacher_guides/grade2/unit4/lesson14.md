Introduce Jump the Line—Add and Subtract within 100

     Standards
Addressing      2.MD.B.6


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 1 of the Jump the Line center. In this stage, students take turns
making strategic choices about numbers to add or subtract in order to reach target numbers. Students choose 3 target
numbers and mark them on the number line. Both players start at 30 on the number line. They take turns spinning all 3
spinners, choosing a number from 1 of the spinners, and moving that distance on the number line. The first player to
land directly on 2 target numbers wins.
