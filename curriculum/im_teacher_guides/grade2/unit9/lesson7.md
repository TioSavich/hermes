# Add and Subtract within 1,000
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U9-L7` — Grade grade2, Unit 9, Lesson 7_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for finding the value of expressions within 1,000 that are more or less challenging.
- Justify (orally and in writing) why sums and differences within 100 may be more or less challenging to find and explain mental strategies.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s add and subtract within 1,000.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to add and subtract within 1,000 using methods based on place value.

Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to add and subtract within 1,000 using methods based on place value.


Narrative
In previous lessons, students practiced composing and decomposing three-digit numbers in different ways. This
lesson connects composing and decomposing numbers to methods for adding and subtracting numbers within 1,000.
The first activity encourages students to consider which sums and differences within 1,000 would be least and most
challenging to find. Students are encouraged to think about what might make finding the values of some expressions
more challenging. They share the methods they use to find these values. The second activity focuses only on sums
within 100 and encourages students to use and share methods that help them fluently find sums and differences.
