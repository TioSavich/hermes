# Reason about Quotients
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U3-L16` — Grade grade5, Unit 3, Lesson 16_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Critique (orally) explanations of strategies used to determine the size of a quotient when dividing a whole number by a unit fraction.
- Explain (orally and in writing) strategies for determining whether a quotient involving a whole number and a unit fraction is greater or less than 1.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s apply what we know about division to make sure our answers make sense.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to find quotients involving a whole number and a unit fraction and assess the reasonableness of their answers.
