# Make 2 Parts
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U5-L1` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 5, Lesson 1_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Comprehend (in spoken language) the term “part” refers to a portion of a total collection.
- Use objects to compose or decompose a number within 9.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s break numbers into parts.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to compose and decompose numbers up to 9 and understand that numbers can be composed and decomposed in more than one way.

Activity 3                                                                                                   25 min

Introduce Check It Off—Add within 10

     Standards
Addressing     K.OA.A.2


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 1 of the Check it Off center. Before beginning, remove cards
showing the numbers 6–10 and set aside. Students take turns picking two number cards (0–5) and find the value of their
sum. Students check off the number that represents the value of the sum (0–10) and then fill in the addition expression
on the recording sheet. The partner who checks off the most numbers wins.

After they participate in the center, students choose any previously introduced stage from these centers:

  • Find the Value of Expressions
  • Bingo
  • Shake and Spill
Students will choose from these centers throughout the section. Keep materials from these centers organized to use
each day.
