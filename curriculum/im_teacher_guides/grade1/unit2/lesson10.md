Activity 1                                                                                                     20 min

Introduce Capture Squares—Add within 10

     Standards
Addressing     1.OA.C.6


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn a new center called Capture Squares. Students roll two number
cubes and find the sum using math tools or mental methods they have developed during the unit. Students connect two
dots that are adjacent to the sum on the gameboard. If that line closes the square, they capture the square and shade it
in with their color. If they cannot draw a line, they roll again. The player to shade in three squares first is the winner.
