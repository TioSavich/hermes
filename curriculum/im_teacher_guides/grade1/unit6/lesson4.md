_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U6-L4` — Grade grade1, Unit 6, Lesson 4_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for adding and comparing numbers within 100.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s practice adding and subtracting.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is to practice adding and subtracting within 100.

Activity 1                                                                                                  20 min

Introduce How Close—Add to 100

     Standards
Addressing     1.NBT.C.4


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 3 of the How Close? center. Each student picks 7 cards and
chooses 4 of them to create 2 two-digit numbers. Each student adds the 2 numbers and writes an equation. The student
whose sum is closest to 100 wins a point for the round. Before playing, students should remove cards that show 10 from
their deck and set them aside.
