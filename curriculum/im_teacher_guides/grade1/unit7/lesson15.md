# Write Times
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U7-L15` — Grade grade1, Unit 7, Lesson 15_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Represent the time shown on an analog clock to the hour and the half hour, using digits.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s write times in hours and half hours.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to understand that times called “half past” are represented as ___:30. Students write the time in hours and half hours to match analog clocks.

In the previous lesson, students connected “half past” the hour to half of an analog clock face. They told time in hours
and half hours on clocks that only had an hour hand. At the end of the lesson, they learned that when it is half past the
hour, the minute hand points to the 6.

In this lesson, students connect half an hour to the digital form ___:30. Students write the time in hours and half hours,
based on analog clocks. They add the digital form to the half-past clock cards.
