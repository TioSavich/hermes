# Center Day 1
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U3-L7` — Grade grade1, Unit 3, Lesson 7_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Find and compare (orally) the values of sums and differences within 10.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s play games to practice adding.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to practice adding within 10.

Introduce Compare—Add and Subtract within 10

     Standards
Addressing      1.OA.C.6


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 1 of the Compare center. Both partners flip over a card with an
addition or subtraction expression within 10. The partner whose expression has the greater value takes both cards. The
game is over when each partner runs out of cards to flip over. The partner with the most cards wins.
