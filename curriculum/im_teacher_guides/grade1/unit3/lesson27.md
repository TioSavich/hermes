Introduce How Close—Subtract from 20

     Standards
Addressing     1.OA.C.6


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 2 of the How Close? center. Students start with 4 number cards
and then choose 2 or 3 of those numbers to subtract from 20 to get as close as possible to 0, the target number. The
student with a difference closest to zero wins a point for the round. Before playing, students should remove cards that
show 10 from their deck and set them aside.

Introduce Compare—Add and Subtract within 20

     Standards
Addressing     1.OA.C.5, 1.OA.C.6


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 2 of the Compare center. Students choose a card with either an
addition or subtraction expression within 20 and compare the values. The partner whose card has the greater value
takes both cards. The game is over when each partner runs out of cards to flip over. The partner with the most cards
wins. After learning this new stage of the Compare center, students then choose from other activities focused on
addition and subtraction.

Students choose from any of the previously introduced stages of the listed centers.

  • Compare
  • How Close?
  • Five in a Row


      Access for Students with Disabilities
      Action and Expression: Develop Expression and Communication. Give students access to manipulatives such as
      connecting cubes, counters, or 10-frames.
      Supports accessibility for: Conceptual Processing, Memory
