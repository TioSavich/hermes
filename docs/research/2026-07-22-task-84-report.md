# Task 84: material mathematics in pass 1

## Implementation

Pass 1 now requests three distinct record layers: checkable claims, catalogued
actions, and material representation mentions. The lesson-window driver passes
the existing `math_action_catalog_prompt` into every pass-1 request, including
the counting, partitioning, iteration, and measurement action vocabulary it
provides.

Representation mentions use exactly five drawable kinds:

- `tape_diagram`
- `number_line`
- `area_circle`
- `area_rectangle`
- `equation_chain`

Each mention retains its contiguous verbatim surface, utterance id, stated
partition count, and stated unit fraction. They are `kind: representation` in
the extraction record and carry their drawable kind separately as
`representation_kind`. The claim masker and its pass-2 ledger explicitly skip
them, so material discourse remains legible rather than being converted to a
claim token.

`representation_render.py` projects those records onto the offline Node drawer
adapter used by the deformation-gallery exporters: `fraction-bars` for tape
diagrams, `number-line`, `hybridization-model` circle/rectangle partition
primitives, and `notation`. It writes SVGs under a run's `representations/`
directory and stores a render receipt on the extraction. That receipt joins the
adjudicated claim ids from the same utterance without borrowing their numbers;
the mention still supplies every drawable value. The sous-rature record and PML
report embed each drawable SVG beside its utterance. Missing partition
counts, inconsistent stated unit fractions, unsupported kinds, absent surfaces,
and non-equation notation return an `undrawable` receipt with the original
surface; no substitute scene is drawn.

## What the widened record could feed next

This task does not change the grounded or sequent layer. The new record could
become candidate input, only after a separate grounding contract is designed:

- A numbered number-line jump or stated counting sequence could propose a
  successor trace for `grounded_arithmetic:successor/2`; the existing counting
  seam is `knowledge/crosswalk/families/cw_counting_claim.pl` and the runnable
  counting-on strategy is `knowledge/strategies/math/sar_add_counting_on.pl`.
- A stated equal partition plus a unit fraction and iteration count could
  propose the already-checkable `iterate_to_whole/2` shape, whose current
  grounding seam is `fraction_iterating:partition_iterate_inverse/2` through
  `hermes/math_claim_checker.pl` and `hermes/math_context.pl`.
- A tape, circle, or rectangle with two stated fraction measures could propose
  a co-measurement candidate for
  `divaded_fractional_units:co_measure_fractions/7`; the current checker and
  renderer seams are `hermes/math_claim_checker.pl` and
  `knowledge/strategies/render/fraction_bars_scene.pl`.
- Measurement-division language can remain a material record now. The nearby
  misconception entries explicitly mark division-as-measurement grounding as
  TODO, so it is not yet an executable operation to promote from this record.

These are possible next seams, not evidence that a picture establishes a
grounded inference. The current implementation records and renders only what
the transcript states.
