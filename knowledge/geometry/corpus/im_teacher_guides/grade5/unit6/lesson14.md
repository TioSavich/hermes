# Representing Fractions on a Line Plot
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U6-L14` — Grade grade5, Unit 6, Lesson 14_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Interpret (orally and in writing) and create line plots with fractions and mixed numbers.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s make a line plot and analyze the data we collect.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to make and interpret line plots displaying fractions in eighths.

Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to make and interpret line plots displaying fractions in eighths.


Narrative
In this lesson, students use fraction arithmetic to solve problems about line plots. Students make line plots by
generating data and using given data, and then they solve problems about the line plots. Students have worked with
these line plots in a previous grade. The new part of the work in this lesson and the next is the level of complexity of the
questions they answer. In this lesson, they perform arithmetic with the fractions and answer questions that involve
thinking about all the data as a whole and certain measurements as a fraction of that whole (MP2).
