# Weekend Investigation
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U6-L21` — Grade grade5, Unit 6, Lesson 21_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Interpret (orally and in writing) information in a line plot that represents fractional data.
- Present fractional data on a line plot.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s find out how students spend free time on the weekend.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to apply their understanding of making line plots and using operations with fractions to analyze data.

The purpose of this activity is for students to think about different activities they might participate in during free time, to
generate data based on the activities, and to define categories for the data. When students collect data and define
categories for the data, they model with mathematics (MP4). Encourage students to convert the time for their activities

and quarters of an hour. Students will further explore further the relationship between time and fractions in the Activity
Synthesis.
