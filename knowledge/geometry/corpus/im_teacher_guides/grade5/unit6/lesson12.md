# Solve Problems
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U6-L12` — Grade grade5, Unit 6, Lesson 12_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Interpret and add and subtract fractions and mixed numbers in context.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s solve more problems by adding and subtracting fractions with unlike denominators.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to solve problems that involve adding and subtracting fractions with unlike denominators.

Narrative
In this lesson, students apply their understanding of addition and subtraction of fractions to solve multi-step problems.
They work with fractions and mixed numbers and make estimates as well as finding the values of sums and differences.
Students find the sums and the differences in ways that make sense to them. They will choose common denominators
and also can use techniques they have seen in earlier lessons, such as adding on to make a whole number.
