Interpret Expressions

     Standards
Addressing      5.NF.B.3


In previous activities, students interpreted diagrams and expressions that represented equal sharing situations. The
purpose of this activity is for students to interpret division expressions without the support of diagrams in order to
deepen their understanding about the relationship between fractions and division expressions. The activity is designed
to highlight the relationship between the number of objects being shared and the numerator, and the number of people
sharing and the denominator (MP7).

      “fraction,” “whole.” Invite students to borrow language from the display as needed, and update it throughout the
      lesson.
