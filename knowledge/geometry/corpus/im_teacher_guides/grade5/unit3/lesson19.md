# Fraction Games
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U3-L19` — Grade grade5, Unit 3, Lesson 19_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally and in writing) strategies for creating multiplication and division expressions with given whole numbers and unit fractions that result in the greatest or least product or quotient.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s multiply and divide with fractions.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to use their understanding of fractions and division to make the largest and smallest expressions using given numbers.

Students work together to write expressions involving a unit fraction divided by a whole number and a whole number

Lesson Synthesis
“Today we looked at the value of different multiplication and division expressions involving unit fractions.”

Display the first expressions from the two activities.

“What numbers will make the value of this expression as large as possible?” (I use the 5 and 6 for the numerators and
the 1 and 2 for the denominators.)

“What numbers will make it as small as possible?” (I use the 1 and 2 for the numerators and the 5 and 6 for
denominators.)

“How are the expressions we wrote for the largest and smallest values the same? How are they different?” (They use the
same numbers but they are in the numerator in one expression and in the denominator in the other.)
