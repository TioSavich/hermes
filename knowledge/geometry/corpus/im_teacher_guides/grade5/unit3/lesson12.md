Addressing        5.NF.B.7.a


In this activity, students notice as the divisor increases for a given dividend, the quotient gets smaller. Students may
recognize and explain the relationship between multiplication and division. For example, they may notice that dividing
one quarter into two equal pieces is the same as finding the product of           . This relationship is not explicitly brought
up in the Activity Synthesis, but if students describe this relationship, connect it to the student work that is discussed, as
appropriate. When students notice a pattern, they look for and express regularity in repeated reasoning (MP8).
