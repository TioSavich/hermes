# Notice and Wonder
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U8-L14` — Grade grade5, Unit 8, Lesson 14_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create (in writing) and present a Notice and Wonder activity.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s create a Notice and Wonder.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to apply their understanding of fractions as division to create a Notice and Wonder activity.
