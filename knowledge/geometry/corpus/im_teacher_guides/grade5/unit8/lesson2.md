# More Multiplication
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U8-L2` — Grade grade5, Unit 8, Lesson 2_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Calculate the product of two multi-digit whole numbers.
- Critique (orally and in writing) a calculation of the product of two multi-digit whole numbers.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s practice using the multiplication algorithm.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to practice using the standard algorithm to multiply multi-digit numbers.
