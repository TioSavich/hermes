# Tell Stories about Our School
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U8-L10` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 8, Lesson 10_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create (orally) story problems about the school community.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s tell math stories about our school.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to develop story problems about their school community.
