# Share Story Problems
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U8-L11` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 8, Lesson 11_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Present (orally) strategies for solving story problems about the school community.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s solve story problems.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to represent and solve story problems about their school community.
