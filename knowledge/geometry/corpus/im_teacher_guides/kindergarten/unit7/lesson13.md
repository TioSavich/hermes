# Describe Solid Shapes around Us
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U7-L13` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 7, Lesson 13_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Describe (orally) examples of solid shapes in the environment, including using positional words.
- Represent solid shapes in the environment with clay.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s find solid shapes.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to notice and describe the locations of solid shapes in their environment.

Addressing      K.G.A.1


The purpose of this activity is for students to identify and describe solid shapes in their environment (MP4, MP6). The
shape walk can occur in many locations, such as a classroom, school, gym, playground, or library. Additional objects may
need to be added to the environment to ensure that there are examples of a variety of solid shapes. Students may

between the shapes. (“This shape has a point like a cone, but it is not a cone.”) Students use their own language to
describe the solid shapes and are not required to use shapes’ names. As students identify solid shapes, encourage them
to describe the location of the object, using positional words, such as “above,” “below,” “beside,” and “next to.”
