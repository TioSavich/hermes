# Identify and Describe Solid Shapes
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U7-L10` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 7, Lesson 10_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Identify (orally) a sphere, a cylinder, a cube, and a cone in known objects in the environment.
- Represent a sphere, a cylinder, a cube, and a cone with clay.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s make and talk about solid shapes.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to identify and describe solid shapes.

Activity 2                                                                                                       10 min

Introduce Geoblocks—Describe and Find

     Standards
Addressing     K.G.B.4


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 3 of the Geoblocks center. Students describe solid shapes,
using their own language. Students provide clues so that their partner can identify the shape out of a set of four to six
solid shapes. While students may correctly name the solid shapes, it is not expected in this lesson. Students may
describe the shapes in many ways. Monitor for students who:
  • Reference a familiar object: “This shape looks like a ball.”
  • Describe attributes or parts of the shape, such as “point” and “circle” for a cone.
  • Use the name of the shape: “This shape is a cube.”
  • Use positional words: “This shape is next to the one that looks like a box.”
The purpose of the Activity Synthesis is to highlight the language students use to describe shapes (MP6).
