# Build Solid Shapes
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U7-L12` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 7, Lesson 12_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Represent rectangular prisms with connecting cubes.
- Represent solid shapes with sticks and clay.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s create solid shapes.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to build solid shapes from components.

Activity 2                                                                                                        15 min

Introduce Build Shapes—Match the Solid Shape

      Standards
Addressing      K.G.B.5


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 3 of the Build Shapes center. In this activity, students work with
their partner to build a rectangular prism, using sticks and clay. Students use the rectangular prism they built in the
previous Activity Synthesis to help them. Then students choose another solid shape to make out of sticks and clay. In
this activity, a Gallery Walk is added to the center so that students can reflect on the shapes made by others.
