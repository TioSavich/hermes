# Story Problems about Shapes
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U7-L5` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 7, Lesson 5_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Choose strategies for solving Add To, Result Unknown and Take From, Result Unknown problems and represent the problem with an equation.
- Match (orally) story problems and equations.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s use equations with story problems.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to solve Add To, Result Unknown and Take From, Result Unknown story problems about pattern blocks.
