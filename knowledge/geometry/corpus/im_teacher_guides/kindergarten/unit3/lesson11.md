# Same Shapes
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U3-L11` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 3, Lesson 11_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Determine pattern blocks that are the same when presented in different orientations.
- Explain (orally) how to determine if two pattern block shapes are the same.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s find out which shapes are the same.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to identify shapes that are the same, regardless of orientation.
