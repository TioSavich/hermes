# Compare and Order Decimals in Different Notations
_Anchor ID: `IM-G4-U4-L5` — Grade grade4, Unit 4, Lesson 5_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for comparing and ordering decimals.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s put decimals in order.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to compare and order fractions and decimals to the hundredths.
