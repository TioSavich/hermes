# Center Day 4
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U6-L21` — Grade grade2, Unit 6, Lesson 21_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Ask (orally) questions that encourage comparing two collections of coins, and compare the values of coin collections.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s compare coin collections and work with shapes.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to compare the value of coin collections and to work with shapes.

Activity 1                                                                                                     15 min

Introduce Would You Rather—Money

     Standards
Addressing     2.MD.C.8


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 1 of the Would You Rather? center. Students compare the
values of two sets of coins. The first partner spins and identifies a group of coins. They ask a question that compares the
coins they spun to a collection of coins they make up. Their partner makes a choice of which collection of coins they
would rather have and explains why in terms of the units.
