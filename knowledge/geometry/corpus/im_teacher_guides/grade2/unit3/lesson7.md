# Center Day 1
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U3-L7` — Grade grade2, Unit 3, Lesson 7_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for finding the unknown number that makes an equation true.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s practice addition and subtraction.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to add and subtract within 100 as well as tell and solve math stories.
