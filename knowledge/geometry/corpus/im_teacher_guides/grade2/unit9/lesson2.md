# Fluency Flip
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U9-L2` — Grade grade2, Unit 9, Lesson 2_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Find an unknown addend and explain (orally) mental strategies.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s add and subtract fluently.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to fluently add and subtract within 20 to find unknown addends.

Addressing     2.OA.B.2

encourages students to decompose numbers and to consider how they can create sums that are equivalent but easier
to find as a mental strategy (MP7). Students use the number cards 0–9 from the previous activity to help them figure out
the missing numbers in the equations.
