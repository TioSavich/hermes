# Center Day 1
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U8-L6` — Grade grade2, Unit 8, Lesson 6_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Choose to count by 2, 5, or 10 and record the numbers said when counting within 1,000.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s skip-count and practice addition and subtraction.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to skip-count by 2, 5, and 10 and to add and subtract within 1,000.

Activity 1                                                                                                     20 min

Introduce Write Numbers—Skip-count by 2, 5, and 10

     Standards
Addressing      2.NBT.A.2


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 4 of the Write Numbers center. Students choose to start with 2,
5, or 10 and take turns skip-counting by that number. They choose whether to write the next one, two, or three
numbers in the sequence on the gameboard. The player who writes the last number on the number path wins.

Consider laminating gameboards or placing them in sheet protectors so they can be used multiple times.
