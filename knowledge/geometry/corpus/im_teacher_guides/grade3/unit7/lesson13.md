# Shapes and Play
_Anchor ID: `IM-G3-U7-L13` — Grade grade3, Unit 7, Lesson 13_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Draw and describe (orally) a design for a real-world rectangular space using constraints related to area and perimeter.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s design a park.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to consider how geometric attributes, perimeter, and area are used when designing a playground.

Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to consider how geometric attributes, perimeter, and area are used when
designing a playground.


Narrative
In previous lessons, students learned how to identify different types of quadrilaterals, find the perimeter of different
shapes, and draw shapes with the same area and different perimeters or the same perimeter and different areas. In this
lesson, students put all of this knowledge together to design a small park with specific features, then describe the area
and perimeter of features in the park. Students also solve area and perimeter problems that involve the features of a
park.
