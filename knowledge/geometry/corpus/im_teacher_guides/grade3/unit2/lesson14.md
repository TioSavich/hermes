# Find the Area of a Figure with Unknown Side Lengths
_Anchor ID: `IM-G3-U2-L14` — Grade grade3, Unit 2, Lesson 14_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Critique (orally and in writing) a strategy for calculating an unknown side length.
- Explain (orally) strategies for calculating the area of a figure composed of rectangles, including figures with unknown side lengths.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s find the area of a figure with unknown side lengths.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to calculate the areas of ungridded figures made of rectangles, including figures with unknown side lengths.

The purpose of this lesson is for students to calculate the areas of ungridded figures made of rectangles, including
figures with unknown side lengths.


Narrative
In previous lessons, students found the areas of figures that were fully gridded with squares, and moved toward figures
without a grid that had all their side lengths labeled. In this lesson, students use the strategies they have learned to
decompose the figures into non-overlapping rectangles. They realize that not all measurements need to be given, and
that some lengths can be determined given the rectangular structure of these figures.

Advancing Student Thinking
If students determine incorrect lengths for the unknown sides, consider asking:
   • “Tell me how you found the unknown side length.”
  • “What strategies from the previous activity could you use to find the unknown side length?”




Lesson Synthesis
Display the figure from the Warm-up.

“Today we learned that we can find unknown side lengths in figures. What is it about the structure of the figures we saw
in this lesson that helps you find unknown side lengths?” (They are made up of rectangles, so if we break them into
rectangles, we can think about how the lengths of opposite sides match.)

“How do you find the areas of these figures after you know the side lengths?” (I break the shape into rectangles I see and
find their areas. Then I add the areas of those rectangles together.)
