# Graph and Answer
_Anchor ID: `IM-G3-U8-L7` — Grade grade3, Unit 8, Lesson 7_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create (orally and in writing) and solve one- and two- step Compare problems, based on data presented in a scaled bar graph.
- Represent collected data, with a scaled bar graph, and explain (orally) how a scale was chosen.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s represent our data on scaled bar graphs and answer questions about the data.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to draw a scaled bar graph to represent a data set with several categories.

Ask and Answer Questions

     Standards
Addressing      3.MD.B.3


The purpose of this activity is for students to ask and answer questions, using their bar graphs from a previous activity.

bar graphs. Then students work with a new partner to use these questions to practice solving one- and two-step “how
many more?” and “how many fewer?” problems, using information presented in scaled bar graphs (MP2).

      take turns sharing their understanding with their partner. Listen for and clarify any questions about the context.
