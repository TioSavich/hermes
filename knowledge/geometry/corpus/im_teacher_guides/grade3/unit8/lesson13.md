# How Many Do You See?
_Anchor ID: `IM-G3-U8-L13` — Grade grade3, Unit 8, Lesson 13_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create (in writing) and present a How Many Do You See? routine involving equal groups.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s create a "How Many Do You See?" routine.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to apply their understanding of equal groups and multiplication to create a How Many Do You See? routine.
