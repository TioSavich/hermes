# Center Day 1
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U7-L8` — Grade grade1, Unit 7, Lesson 8_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create and describe (orally) a three-dimensional object composed of smaller three-dimensional shapes.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s work with shapes and add or subtract numbers.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to work with two-dimensional and three-dimensional shapes as well as to practice addition and subtraction.
