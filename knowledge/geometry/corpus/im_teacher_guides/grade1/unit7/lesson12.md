Introduce Can You Draw It—Grade 1 Shapes

     Standards
Addressing     1.G.A.1


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 1 of the Can You Draw It? center. Students describe and draw
two-dimensional shapes. One student secretly picks a shape card and describes the shape to their partner, who draws
it, based on the description. Together they compare the shape that was drawn to the shape on the card. If the student
has drawn the shape correctly, they keep the card. The partner with the most cards at the end of the game wins. Before
playing, remove any cards that do not show a triangle or quadrilateral.


Required Materials
Materials to Gather                                           Materials to Copy
  • Materials from a previous lesson: Activity 1                • Centimeter Dot Paper - Standard (1 copy for every 1
                                                                  students): Activity 1


Required Preparation
  • Each group of 2 students needs a set of triangles and quadrilaterals from the flat shapes cards used during a
    previous lesson. Remove the cards that do not show a triangle or a quadrilateral.
