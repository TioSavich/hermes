# Half of the Clock
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U7-L14` — Grade grade1, Unit 7, Lesson 14_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Comprehend (in spoken and written language) the meaning of the phrase “half past.”
- Interpret (orally) an analog clock and determine the time to the hour and half hour.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s learn more about time to the half hour.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to learn about the position of the hands on an analog clock at half past the hour.

Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to learn about the position of the hands on an analog clock at half past the
hour.


Narrative
In a previous lesson, students learned how to tell and write the time in hours. In a previous section, students partitioned
shapes into two equal pieces and identified each piece as “a half of” the shape.

In this lesson, students learn about the position of the hands on an analog clock at half past the hour. They analyze how
the hands on the clock move as time goes from one hour to the next. They describe the time as “half past” the hour
when the hour hand is halfway between two numbers and the minute hand is pointing at the 6. They create new clock
cards that show half past the hour and add the minute hand to the clocks.

Notice and Wonder: The Hands on the Clock Go Round and Round

     Standards                                                       Instructional Routines
Addressing     1.MD.B.3                                          • Notice and Wonder


The purpose of this activity is for students to connect their understanding of half of a circle to the minute hand moving
halfway around the face of a clock (MP7). Students watch a display clock to see that as the minute hand goes around the
clock, the hour hand goes from one hour to the next. In the Activity Synthesis, students add the minute hand to their
half-past clock cards. In the next lesson, students will connect the term “half past” to 30 minutes to write times in hours
and half hours.

Use a display clock set to 10:00 to move the minute hand around until the clock shows 11:00. If a display clock is not
available, this video can be used.
