# Sums of 10
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U3-L4` — Grade grade1, Unit 3, Lesson 4_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create sums that have a value of 10 and explain (orally) strategies for finding all of the possible sums of 10.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s find all the ways to make 10.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is to build toward fluency within 10 by looking for and making use of patterns in sums that have a value of 10.
