# Methods for Addition within 20
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U3-L19` — Grade grade1, Unit 3, Lesson 19_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally and using words or other representations) strategies for adding within 20.
- Interpret (orally and using words or other representations) strategies for adding within 20, including those based on properties of operations and creating equivalent but easier or known sums.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s add within 20.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to analyze addition methods for adding within 20, then use those methods flexibly to find sums based on the numbers in a given expression.

Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to analyze addition methods for adding within 20, then use those methods
flexibly to find sums based on the numbers in a given expression.


Narrative
In previous lessons, students decomposed an addend in order to make a ten and thought about sums as equivalent
       expressions. They applied the commutative and associative properties to find the sum more easily. Students
looked for and used patterns in addition expressions, such as            . In this lesson, students continue adding
within 20.

Students may use any method they choose, but they are encouraged to think about methods which may work better for
certain sums. In the first activity, students analyze three different methods for finding a sum, all of which include
decomposing an addend to make a known sum. In the second activity, students find sums within 20 and share their
thinking with a partner.
