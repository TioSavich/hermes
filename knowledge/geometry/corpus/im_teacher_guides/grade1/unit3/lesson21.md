# Center Day 3
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U3-L21` — Grade grade1, Unit 3, Lesson 21_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for adding 2 and 3 addends within 20.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s play games where we add.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to practice adding within 20.

Introduce How Close—Add to 20

     Standards
Addressing         1.OA.C.6


The purpose of this lesson is for students to learn Stage 1 of the How Close? center. Students start with 5 number cards.
For each round, they choose 3 of their cards to write an addition expression with three addends that has a value as
close as possible to 20, the target number. The student with a sum closest to 20 gets a point for that round.

The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 3 of the Five in a Row center. Students pick a card and decide
to add 7, 8, or 9 to the number on their card. They then place a counter on the sum on the gameboard. If a player can’t
make a sum that is uncovered on the gameboard, they pick another card. The game ends when a player gets 5 counters
in a row or the board is filled.

The gameboard will be used again in upcoming lessons. Consider copying it on cardstock or laminating it for future use.
