# The Garden
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U3-L28` — Grade grade1, Unit 3, Lesson 28_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Ask (orally and in writing) questions about a real- world situation and identify which questions can be answered with addition or subtraction.
- Create (in writing) and solve story problems that involve addition or subtraction within 20.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s write and solve story problems.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to ask questions about a real-world situation that can be answered using mathematics.

Narrative
This lesson is optional because it does not address any new mathematical content standards. This lesson does provide
students with an opportunity to apply precursor skills of mathematical modeling (MP4).

Students begin by wondering about different aspects of gardens. They then determine which of their questions can be
answered using addition or subtraction. Students write story problems that will lead to answers to these questions.
Finally, they solve each other’s story problems, answering the questions that were asked earlier.
