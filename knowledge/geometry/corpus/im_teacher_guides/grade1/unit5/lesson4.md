# Center Day 1
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U5-L4` — Grade grade1, Unit 5, Lesson 4_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for finding the number that makes equations with two-digit numbers true.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s add within 100.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to practice adding within 100 without composing a ten.

Introduce Number Puzzles—Within 100 without Composing

     Standards
Addressing     1.NBT.C.4, 1.OA.D.8


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 2 of the Number Puzzles center. Students work together to use
digit cards to make addition and subtraction equations true. Students add and subtract within 100 without composing.
Each digit (0–9) can only be used once in a puzzle. Puzzles with less than ten spaces will have leftover cards.
