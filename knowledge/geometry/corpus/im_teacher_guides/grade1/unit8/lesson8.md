# Show Me All the Ways
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U8-L8` — Grade grade1, Unit 8, Lesson 8_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Compare and contrast (orally) different base-ten representations of the same two-digit number.
- Represent the same two-digit number in different ways.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s represent 2-digit numbers in different ways.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to represent two-digit numbers in different ways to demonstrate place value understanding.

Gallery Walk: Our Favorite Numbers

     Standards
Addressing     1.NBT.B


The purpose of this activity is for students to interpret representations of numbers up to 100. Students participate in a
Gallery Walk and find representations that are the same and different from their own. If time permits, students can
return to their own work and revise based on what they saw in their classmates’ work. As students look through each
others’ work, they discuss how the representations are the same and different and can defend different points of view
(MP3).
