# Center Day 1
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U2-L5` — Grade grade1, Unit 2, Lesson 5_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create (orally) and solve story problems involving addition and subtraction.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s play games to practice adding and subtracting.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to tell and solve story problems, and add and subtract within 10.

Activity 1                                                                                                     15 min

Introduce Math Stories—Add and Subtract

     Standards
Addressing     1.OA.A.1


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 4 of the Math Stories center. One student poses an addition or
subtraction story problem about a picture. Their partner represents and solves the story problem. Pages of picture
books can also be used to help students generate stories.

The recording sheet includes directions and a line for recording an equation to represent each problem. Students do not
need to write equations at this point in the unit. To avoid this distraction or possible confusion, consider using the
recording sheet from Math Stories, Stage 3 until Section D.
