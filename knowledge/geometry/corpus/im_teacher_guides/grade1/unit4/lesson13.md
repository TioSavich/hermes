# Center Day 2
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U4-L13` — Grade grade1, Unit 4, Lesson 13_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for adding and subtracting within 20, including making a 10.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s add and subtract and work with tens and ones.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to add and subtract within 20 and work with two-digit numbers.
