# Center Day 2
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U6-L10` — Grade grade1, Unit 6, Lesson 10_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Choose and use length units to estimate and measure the length of objects.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s measure the length of objects.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to practice measuring and adding within 100.
