# Expressions and Story Problems
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U4-L14` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 4, Lesson 14_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Interpret (orally) a subtraction expression that represents a Take From, Result Unknown problem.
- Match (orally) expressions and Add To or Take, From, Result Unknown problems.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s match expressions with story problems.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to connect expressions to story problems.
