# Draw Groups of Things
_Anchor ID: `IM-GK-U2-L17` — Grade kindergarten, Unit 2, Lesson 17_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Draw groups to represent a given written number.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s figure out how many things to draw.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to draw a given number of images.

The purpose of this activity is for students to draw images to match a given number. Students work in small groups to
draw many different groups with the number on their poster. After students create their posters, they participate in a
Gallery Walk. Consider establishing a small sound or motion to signal to students when it is time to move from one
poster to the next.

This activity uses MLR7 Compare and Connect. Advances: Representing, Conversing.
