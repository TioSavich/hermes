# Center Day 2
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U7-L11` — Grade grade2, Unit 7, Lesson 11_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for adding three-digit numbers that may require composing units when adding by place.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s add numbers within 1,000.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to practice adding numbers within 1,000.

Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to practice adding numbers within 1,000.


Narrative
This lesson is optional because it is an opportunity for extra practice that not all classes may need. In Activity 1, students
learn Stage 4 of the How Close? center. In this new stage, called How Close—Add to 1,000, students pick 8 digit cards
and then choose 6 of them to make an expression that yields a number as close as possible to the target number, 1,000.
In Activity 2, students learn Stage 8 of the Five in a Row: Addition and Subtraction center. In this new stage, students add
within 1,000, with composing. Then they choose to continue working on Five in a Row: Addition and Subtraction, or
choose between two previously introduced centers that focus on addition with three-digit numbers.
