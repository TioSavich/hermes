# Book Drive
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U7-L19` — Grade grade2, Unit 7, Lesson 19_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Create (using words and other formats) a representation to organize information.
- Explain (orally) how to use known information to solve real-world problems involving addition and subtraction.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s organize information about a book drive.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to use addition and subtraction within 1,000 to organize information and answer questions about a book drive.

The purpose of this lesson is for students to use addition and subtraction within 1,000 to organize information and
answer questions about a book drive.


Narrative
This lesson is optional because it does not address any new mathematical content standards. This lesson does provide
students with an opportunity to apply precursor skills of mathematical modeling (MP4).

In previous lessons, students added and subtracted within 1,000. In this lesson, students use these skills to organize
information about the books collected and donated during a book drive. Students organize the information in any way
that makes sense to them, and decide when to add or subtract in order to keep track of how many books the school has
as they collect and donate books during each month of the book drive. Students then answer additional questions
about the book drive that require them to make their own decisions about the book drive.

Questions about the Book Drive

      Standards
Addressing       2.NBT.B.7


The purpose of this activity is for students to use addition and subtraction to answer questions about the book-drive
context introduced in the previous activity. Students identify the information they need to figure out how many books
the school collected in May. They then choose a number they think is reasonable and solve the problem. Students then
consider what the school could do with the books they still have when the book drive is over. When students identify
information they need to know to solve a problem and consider assumptions about information not given in a situation,
they model with mathematics (MP4).
