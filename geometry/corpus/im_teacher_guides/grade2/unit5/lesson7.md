# Center Day 1
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U5-L7` — Grade grade2, Unit 5, Lesson 7_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Describe (orally) and identify three-digit numbers using clues based on place value.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s use place value to identify numbers and practice adding and subtracting.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to work with place value and practice addition and subtraction within 100.

students choose to continue working on Mystery Number, or choose between two previously introduced centers
