Introduce Estimate and Measure—Centimeters, Inches, and Feet

     Standards
Addressing      2.MD.A.1, 2.MD.A.3


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 2 of the Estimate and Measure center. Students choose an
object and a unit to measure the object. Students can choose from centimeters, inches, and feet. They estimate the
length of the object and then measure to find the actual length to the nearest whole unit. In this activity, students also
find the difference between their estimate and their measurement for extra practice.

Activity 2                                                                                                     20 min

Introduce Target Measurements—Inches and Centimeters

     Standards
Addressing      2.MD.A.1, 2.MD.A.3, 2.MD.A.4


The purpose of this activity is for students to learn Stage 1 of the Target Measurements center. In this stage, students try
to draw a line segment as close as possible to the length of the target measurement in whole inches or centimeters.
They measure the line to the nearest whole unit and find the difference between the target measurement and the
actual length to get a score. The student with the lower score after 8 rounds wins.
