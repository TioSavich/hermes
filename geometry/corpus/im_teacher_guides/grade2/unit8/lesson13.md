# Center Day 2
_Anchor ID: `IM-G2-U8-L13` — Grade grade2, Unit 8, Lesson 13_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) how to determine if equations with equal addends are true or false.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s skip-count and practice addition and subtraction.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to skip-count by 2, 5, and 10 and to add and subtract within 1,000.
