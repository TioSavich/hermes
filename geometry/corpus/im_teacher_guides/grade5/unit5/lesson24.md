# Divide Decimals by Whole Numbers
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U5-L24` — Grade grade5, Unit 5, Lesson 24_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Calculate quotients of a decimal to the hundredths divided by a whole number and explain (orally and in writing) strategies used.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s divide decimals by whole numbers.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to divide a decimal less than 1 by a whole number.
