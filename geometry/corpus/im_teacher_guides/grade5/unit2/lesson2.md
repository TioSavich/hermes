# Share More Sandwiches
_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U2-L2` — Grade grade5, Unit 2, Lesson 2_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Match (orally) diagrams, division expressions, and fractions that represent the same situation.
- Use a whole-number division expression to represent a story problem with a solution that is a fraction.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s use diagrams and expressions to represent division situations.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to relate equal shares to division expressions and visual representations of fractions.
