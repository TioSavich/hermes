_Anchor ID: `IM-G5-U3-L14` — Grade grade5, Unit 3, Lesson 14_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Compare and contrast (orally and in writing) diagrams and equations that represent dividing a whole number by a fraction.
- Generalize (orally and in writing) that .

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let's solve problems by dividing a whole number by a unit fraction.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to solve problems involving division of a whole number by a unit fraction and write equations to represent them.

fraction.”

fraction in a whole number because if it is 1 divided by a unit fraction, there will be however many unit fractions that
make up 1 whole. So, that will be a whole number of unit fractions.)
