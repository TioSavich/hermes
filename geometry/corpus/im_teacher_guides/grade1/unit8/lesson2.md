# Relate Addition and Subtraction
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U8-L2` — Grade grade1, Unit 8, Lesson 2_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) how to determine the values that make equations within 10 true, including using the relationship between addition and subtraction.
- Identify differences within 10 that are known and not yet known and explain (orally) how to use a related sum to determine the value of differences that are not yet known.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s look at the relationship between addition and subtraction.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to use the relationship between addition and subtraction to fluently add and subtract within 10.

Addressing     1.OA.C.6


The purpose of this activity is for students to build an understanding of the relationship between addition and
subtraction (MP7). By using two different colored cubes, students can see the two parts that make the total. They can
also see when one part is removed from the total, the other part remains. Students write addition and subtraction
equations to represent each cube tower.

Differences I Know

     Standards
Addressing     1.OA.C.6


The purpose of this activity is for students to identify the differences within 10 they do not yet know from memory.
Students write these differences on index cards which can be used for students to build fluency. It is not important for
students to accurately distinguish between sums they recall from memory and those they find with fluency using a
strategy. The focus of the lesson should be identifying the differences that take students longer to find and to develop
strategies for finding the value of these differences with increased fluency. Students will continue to practice recalling
sums and differences within 10 from memory in grade 2.
