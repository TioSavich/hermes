# Center Day 3
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U5-L13` — Grade grade1, Unit 5, Lesson 13_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies for adding 2 two-digit numbers that may require composing a ten.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s add 2 two-digit numbers.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to practice adding 2 two-digit numbers with composing a ten.
