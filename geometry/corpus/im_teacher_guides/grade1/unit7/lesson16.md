# Hard Times
_Anchor ID: `IM-G1-U7-L16` — Grade grade1, Unit 7, Lesson 16_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Critique (orally and using words or other representations) arguments about how to read the time on an analog clock.
- Interpret and represent times to the hour and the half hour, using digital and analog clocks.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s show time in hours and half hours.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to show and write the time in hours and half hours.

Sunday Schedule

     Standards
Addressing     1.MD.B.3

schedule. Students fill in all the blanks in a schedule—the time, the activity, and the clock—to create their ideal Sunday
schedule. The task gives an opportunity for students to relate time and telling time to their Sunday schedule (MP2).
