# Answer Questions about Scaled Bar Graphs
_Anchor ID: `IM-G3-U1-L7` — Grade grade3, Unit 1, Lesson 7_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Explain (orally) strategies used to solve one-step Compare problems within 100 based on data presented in scaled bar graphs.
- Interpret scaled bar graphs and ask (in writing) questions that can be answered by the data in a scaled bar graph.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s solve problems based on data represented in bar graphs.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to solve one-step “how many more?” and “how many fewer?” problems based on data presented in a scaled bar graph.
