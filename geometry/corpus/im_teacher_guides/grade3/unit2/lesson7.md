# Different Square Units (Part 2)
_Anchor ID: `IM-G3-U2-L7` — Grade grade3, Unit 2, Lesson 7_

## Learning Goals (teacher-facing)
- Comprehend (in spoken and in written language) the meaning of the terms “square foot” and “square meter.”
- Explain (orally and in writing) whether a given area would be measured with square centimeters, square inches, square feet, or square meters.

## Student-Facing Learning Goals
Let’s learn about larger square units.

## Lesson Purpose
The purpose of this lesson is for students to learn about the square foot and the square meter, and consider when these larger units can be helpful to measure area.

“Now, we’ve worked with different types of square units. Why is it important to have many different options rather than
