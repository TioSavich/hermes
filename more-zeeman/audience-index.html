One capability is deliberately not here: the teaching console reads student work, so it is never hosted publicly. To run it on your own machine: bash hermes/app/launch.sh, then open http://127.0.0.1:8765/. The interactive visualizers that draw a strategy live render through that same local server; the pages above are static and make no calls to any language model.
Part of umedcta-formalization, the Prolog and manuscript archive for Understanding Mathematics as an Emancipatory Discipline: A Critical Theory Approach. The model covers whole-number arithmetic through the crisis cycle; for fractions it draws the area model and the rules-without-reason contrast, and it is honest about what it cannot yet model.
