For contributors: modules described here but not loaded

crosswalk/merge_evidence.pl is the provenance-gate ledger for approved canonical-concept mappings. The merge check reads it during development; it is not a runtime crosswalk operation.

geometry/geometry_bridge.pl is the standalone loader used to compare the geometry closure with the worker closure. It stays outside the worker by design.

strategies/render/rigid_motion_scene.pl compiles translation, reflection, and rotation specifications into the shared render contract. The worker loads it import-free; this page reaches it through the existing geometry operation.

pml/talkmoves_adapter.pl prepares attributed TalkMoves packets for review. pml/audit_connectors.pl audits declared MUA connectors against executable practices. pml/mua_health.pl reports which MUA relations have runnable demonstrations. pml/axioms_eml.pl records the toggle-gated embodied-modal transition axioms. pml/mua_conjectures.pl is the intentionally empty register for relations not yet demonstrated. These modules remain outside the startup closure.

misconceptions/pml_wire.pl is wired into the worker and the console's misconception area. It reports the recorded source-tag-to-PML connections generated from CONNECTS-TO annotations in the misconception registry.
