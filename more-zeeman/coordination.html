The Spatial Synapse: Coordinating & Fractalizing

This unified canvas demonstrates the exact **structural identity** between base arithmetic and fractions. When you run `7 + 8` alongside `7/10 + 8/10`, the **make-base** move fires simultaneously across both dimensions. On the left (Ace of Base), units are grouped into a solid Rod of size $B$. On the right (Fraction Bars), fraction partitions of size $1/D$ flow to complete a Whole unit bar ($1.0$). Varying the base to 12 demonstrates how the exact same CGI strategy shape governs the regrouping move, with only the numeral base target changing.
