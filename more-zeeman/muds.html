I.What this page is⋆

Brandom's MUR applied to a registry of strategies

In Between Saying and Doing, Brandom names a small set of Meaning-Use Relations between practices-or-abilities (P) and vocabularies (V). The atomic ones are PV-sufficiency (deploying a practice suffices to deploy a vocabulary), VP-sufficiency (a vocabulary suffices to specify a practice), and PP-sufficiency (one practice suffices to algorithmically elaborate another). Stacked together, these give the resultant LX relation: Vmeta is elaborated-explicating for Vbase when the practices of Vmeta elaborate the practices of Vbase and make explicit, as expressible content, what those practices carry implicitly.

The page renders the corresponding facts in pml/mua_relations.pl as Meaning-Use Diagrams. The runnable cross-multiplication and area-model version lives on Where it breaks.

II.The Diagram⋆

The canonical Brandomian MUD is a square: two practices (P, boxes) on one side, two vocabularies (V, ovals) on the other. The atomic arrows are numbered; the resultant — the relation that is composed out of the atoms — is drawn as a dashed accent line labeled Res, with the recipe of atoms it composes. LX is one such resultant.

III.Grounding metaphors⋆

Where each metaphor breaks

Lakoff and Núñez identify four grounding metaphors, systematic mappings from bodily activity to arithmetic, and several repair metaphors (Lakoff and Núñez, Where Mathematics Comes From). The break points are kept as first-class queryable facts in formalization/grounding_metaphors.pl rather than as commentary; each card below lists what the metaphor cannot ground and which repair metaphor (if any) covers the inference instead. The little illustration at the top of each card is the metaphor's source-domain image, the physical situation the metaphor asks you to think with.

What this page does not do

The freshman and math-ed registers are stubbed. The toggle buttons exist and the routing works; the prose for those audiences has not been written. Selecting either button replaces the philosopher prose with a short note saying so. The diagram and the metaphor cards are register-independent because the underlying facts are.

The dataset is a seed, not an exhaustive map. The 32 practices, 24 vocabularies, and 13 LX edges visible here were curated against the action-automata registry and against LK_RB_Synthesis's strategy_metadata; they are not a comprehensive registry of arithmetic practice. Adding new practice/2, vocabulary/2, and lx_for/3 facts in pml/mua_relations.pl and rerunning scripts/research/export_mua_for_mud.py regenerates the JSON the page reads.

The page is a static rendering, not a live query into Prolog. It reads mua_data.json, which is produced from the live registry by the export script. When the registry changes and the export script is rerun, the page updates; until then, the page shows the snapshot. The harness's --check flag verifies the JSON matches the current Prolog state.
